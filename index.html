About US

Dear Friends, we know Service to cow (Go-Seva) is given the highest place in the Vedas and Cow is treated as ‘mother’. As members of the reconstituted Sri Venugopal Swamy Development committee, we framed few objectives to ensure comfortable living conditions to Cows by providing shelter, organic grass feed along with special care of the calves to maintain a steady growth. The dry, green grass and dhana (Cow Feed) are fed to the cows every day. Regular check-up by the veterinary doctor has been arranged. Water sources, Electricity and infrastructure of the temple and its surroundings have been provided.

Presently shelter has been provided to around 40+ cows, and regularly living conditions of the cows are being monitored, with inadequate self- sustaining budget.

As a members, with an intention to serve the abandoned cows, we request your support to donate to the “Go-Seva”. Dear friends, No other donation can be compared with cow donation, the practice of donation for Go-Seva receives eternal blessing as he is blessed by all the gods.
